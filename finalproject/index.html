Dead & Company

Dead & Company is an American rock band. It formed in 2015 with a lineup of former Grateful Dead member Bob Weir (guitar and vocals), Mickey Hart (drums), and Bill Kreutzmann (drums), along with John Mayer (guitars and vocals), Oteil Burbridge (bass, percussion, and vocal), and Jeff (keyboards). Dead & Company primarily perform Grateful Dead covers and is credited with popularizing Grateful Dead music to a younger audience. In 2023, Jay Lane replaced Kreutzmann as the band's second drummer for their final tour. Their final tour was attended by over 840,000 fans with the final show taking place in San Francisco, the birthplace of the Grateful Dead. retrieved from Dead & Company wikipedia
